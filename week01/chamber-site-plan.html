Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Alajuela Chamber of Commerce, which will be representing Alajuela city in Costa Rica

Site Purpose

The main purpose of this chamber is to promote commerce from local businesses in Alajuela. People from this city will be able to show their talents, skills and exchange their ideas.

Scenarios

  • How can I join this community?
  • I'm looking for talent from this city, where can I find more information?
  • I want people to know my entrepreneurship, is this the right place?
